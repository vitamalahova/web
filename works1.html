ITEMS OF A LITTLE VALUE \ mask theatre, 2020, LV

Directing: Vita Malahova Performers: Aurelia Pronona, Darta Dušele, Guna Birža, Madara Luīze Musician, Vita Malahova, Edgars Lipors Premiere: Vides Dejas Festival, August 2020, Latvia


ITEMS OF A LITTLE VALUE is a tribute to the vanishing fieldwork and tools. The idea came from visiting abandoned farmsteads in rural areas, which are a kind of museum, an encyclopedia of bygone times, and people. The performance is created in the aesthetics of physical theater, combining dance and mask theater.

ASK THE BREAD \ ongoing theatre project

piloted by CuntsCollective and I Patom Theatre, 2019-2020 Latvia
Vita Malahova (idea, concept, co-director, acting), Tone Haldrup Lorenzen (staging), Aude Lorrillard (acting), Vika Eksta (visual art), Alberto Barberis (music).

The performance takes its primary inspiration from the self-portrait project God Nature Toil of the Latvian photographer Vika Eksta. Collaboration with: Residency center SERDE, residency center Rucka, Gertrudes Street Theatre, Sansusī - Wellbeing residency program, Riga Circus Residency.

I RAN AND GOT TIRED \ physical theatre

developed by I Patom Theatre, directed by Magnus Errboe, 2017 Italy-Denmark
Vita Malahova and Aude Lorrillard (idea, concept, acting), Magnus Errboe (staging), Alberto Barberis (music).

I Ran And Got Tired is a poetic treatment of the life of the Russian author Daniil Kharms. It is a physical, multilingual and vibrant performance at the meeting point between sound, text and movement.

COLORFUL TALES \ theatre for children

developed by I Patom Theatre, produced by Astragali Teatro 2018 Italy
Vita Malahova and Aude Lorrillard (idea, concept, acting)

Thanks to the figure of the clown, a lovable, innocent, clumsy and nostalgic being, Vita Malahova and Aude Lorrillard bring on stage some of the Colorful Tales of the Latvian author Imants Ziedonis.
